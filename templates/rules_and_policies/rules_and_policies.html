HawkSpot Rules and Policies

    Rooms may be reserved from 30 minutes up to 2 hours at a time. Reservations are accepted up to 14 days in advance. Please limit to one reservation per person per day. Rooms should not be booked for job interviews or TA hours; please contact your department for those services. The personâ€™s name that is under the reservation must check-in at the service desk with an IIT ID card at the start of the reservation. Reservations will be cancelled if the group is more than 15 minutes late for the reserved room and time. Dry-erase markers, erasers, and cables for presentation equipment are available for check out at the Information Desk. The person that made the reservation is in charge of the condition of the room. Do not move library furniture into study rooms or study room furniture out. Food and covered drinks are permitted. Be considerate of those using the room after your group and clean up after your group. Catered events are not permitted without prior approval. Rooms are not soundproof. Please be considerate. Voices should be kept at low, conversational levels. Do not leave the room unattended. The library is not responsible for the loss or theft of personal property. Items left unattended will be placed in Galvin Library lost and found. Library staff will check the condition of each room between groups.
